All about Brad

Brad Copenhaver grew up in rural Pennsylvania and now lives in Portland, Oregon. He has traveled the world, lived abroad, and watched every episode of the first twelve seasons of The Simpsons. He loves to write about himself in the third person, and his favorite color is orange.

Brad earned a master's degree in Human-Computer Interaction from Carnegie Mellon University in 2009. The program was a joint program with the University of Madeira and involved classes at both universities. In case you didn't know, Madeira is a Portuguese, subtropical island in the Atlantic Ocean, and Brad lived there for a year while he was studying.

Way back in the early 2000's, Brad attended Washington and Jefferson College and earned a Bachelor of Science in Psychology. He also minored in Math and IT: New Media studies. His time at W&J taught Brad a lot about life away from home, meeting new people, and working together. Of course that was so long ago that most of those lessons have been replaced with more mature and jaded iterations.
